Sushi Kokku can be found in three locations along the central coast: San Luis Obispo, Paso Robles, and Pismo Beach. It's niche is in keeping things streamlined, offering affordable, high quality sushi. Sushi Kokku serves as both a convenient take out and eat-in restaurant. With its sleek furnishing and relaxed atmosphere, Sushi Kokku promises a delightful experience.
