London Fog Tea Latte

Recipe Lesson Comments
4.5 from 1 review

total cook time: 10-12 minutes

yield: 1 serving (approx. 12 ounces)

ingredients:

serving: 1x 2x 3x
  • 1 tbps. earl grey loose leaf // 1 earl grey tea bag
  • 2 tps. dried lavender (optional)
  • 1-2 tps. brown / white sugar (or sweetner preference)
  • 1/4 tps. vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of hot water
  • 2 tbps. earl grey loose leaf // 2 earl grey tea bags
  • 2 tps. dried lavendar (optional)
  • 2-4 tps. brown / white sugar (or sweetner preference)
  • 1/2 tps. vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of hot water
  • 3 tbps. earl grey loose leaf // 3 earl grey tea bags
  • 2-3 tps. dried lavendar (optional)
  • 3-5 tps. brown / white sugar (or sweetner preference)
  • 3/4 tps. vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 1/2 cups of hot water

note: can be served warm or over ice

preparation:

tea / lavender: tablespoon(s) teaspoon(s) gram(s)
sugar: teaspoon(s) tablespoon(s) gram(s)
liquid ingredients: cup(s) milliliter(s) tablespoon(s)

instructions:

  • Bring water to boil. Add 1 tbps. tea and 2 tps. lavender to 1/2 cup of water. Steep for 10 minutes.
  • Heat 3/4 cup of milk on the stovetop. Make sure milk does not come to a boil. * skip to step 3 if iced is preferred
  • Strain tea leaves and lavender. Pour into a cup.* add ice to this step if preferred cold
  • Stir in 1/4 tps. of vanilla extract, 1-2 tps. of sweetner of preference, and milk.
  • Garnish with lavender leaves (optional).
  • Ready to serve.
